# Personal Coach Skill

## Overview

**Personal Coach** identifies strategic work and career patterns from your conversation, then suggests one high-leverage weekly behavior change and a lightweight tracker to help you act on it.

## What This Skill Does

This skill is designed for practical coaching rather than general motivation. It focuses on:

- spotting recurring behavior patterns
- identifying strengths and blind spots
- proposing one focused weekly experiment
- giving you a simple table and CSV tracker

## Sample Prompts For Testing

### Weekly Coaching Insight

```text
Based on our conversation, identify one pattern in how I work that is helping me and one that is holding me back.
Then give me one behavior change to test this week.
```

### Communication Coaching

```text
Coach me on my communication style.
What pattern do you see, what is the likely downside, and what is one practical experiment I should run this week?
```

### Career Opportunity

```text
Look at the way I describe my current work challenges.
What hidden strength or opportunity am I underusing, and how should I test it over the next 7 days?
```

### Decision-Making Pattern

```text
Analyze my decision-making pattern from this conversation.
Show me one thing I should change this week and give me a tracker to measure whether it helped.
```

## Typical Outputs

- one high-leverage weekly coaching insight
- a What / Why / How action table
- daily and weekly tracking suggestions
- downloadable CSV tracker
