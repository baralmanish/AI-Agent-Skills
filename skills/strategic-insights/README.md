# Strategic Insights Skill

## Overview

**Strategic Insights** identifies under-leveraged professional opportunities from your conversation and turns them into one concrete weekly change with simple tracking.

## What This Skill Does

This skill focuses on strategic professional growth. It looks for patterns in your thinking, work habits, or leadership behavior and then helps you act on one meaningful insight.

It typically provides:

- one core strategic insight
- one weekly behavior change
- a What / Why / How table
- a lightweight CSV tracker

## Sample Prompts For Testing

### Find the Missed Opportunity

```text
Based on this conversation, what strategic opportunity am I overlooking in my work?
Give me one weekly behavior change to test it.
```

### Leadership Insight

```text
Analyze how I am approaching leadership in this discussion.
What pattern should I strengthen, what pattern should I change, and how should I test that this week?
```

### Professional Growth Pattern

```text
Find one professional growth pattern in how I describe my work.
Turn that into a concrete What / Why / How action plan and tracker.
```

### Role Strategy Coaching

```text
Use the conversation below to identify one hidden leverage point in my current role.
Then give me a simple way to track whether I am improving it this week.
```

## Typical Outputs

- one actionable strategic insight
- one weekly experiment or behavior change
- simple tracking table
- downloadable CSV tracker
