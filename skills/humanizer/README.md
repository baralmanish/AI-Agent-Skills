# Humanizer Skill

## Overview

**Humanizer** rewrites AI-generated text so it sounds natural, specific, and written by a real person rather than by a template-heavy assistant.

## What This Skill Does

Use this skill when text sounds robotic, overly polished, repetitive, or full of common AI phrasing. It focuses on:

- removing obvious AI tone
- improving rhythm and sentence variety
- replacing generic wording with clearer language
- making the voice feel more confident and human

## Sample Prompts For Testing

### Humanize an Email

```text
Humanize the email draft below.
Keep the meaning the same, but make it sound natural and less like AI wrote it.
```

### Rewrite a Report Paragraph

```text
Rewrite the paragraph below so it sounds more human, direct, and believable.
Remove corporate filler and AI-style phrasing.
```

### Remove AI Tone

```text
This article draft sounds artificial.
Make it sound like a real person wrote it, while keeping the ideas intact.
```

### Make It Conversational

```text
Take the text below and make it more conversational and readable.
Do not make it sloppy. Just make it sound normal and human.
```

## Typical Outputs

- a direct rewritten version of the original text
- fewer stock phrases and less boilerplate tone
- more natural sentence flow and wording
