# Expense Analyzer — Quick Reference

## Trigger Phrases

```
"Analyze my spending"
"Here's my credit card statement — categorize it"
"What am I spending too much on?"
"Review my subscriptions"
"Build me a budget dashboard"
"Show me a savings projection"
```

## What to Provide

- Paste a credit card statement, bank export, or transaction list
- CSV, text table, or even a screenshot description works
- More months of data = better trend analysis

## Output Levels

| Output                  | What It Shows                                              |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Spending summary        | Category totals, top merchants, month-over-month           |
| Subscription audit      | All recurring charges, flagged forgotten/low-value ones    |
| Budget dashboard (HTML) | Interactive category breakdown, trend charts               |
| What-if tools           | Adjust spending in one category, see monthly/annual impact |
| Savings projection      | How small changes compound over 6 months, 1 year, 5 years  |

## Spending Categories

Housing · Food & Dining · Transportation · Health · Shopping · Entertainment · Travel · Education · Personal Care · Financial · Subscriptions · Children & Family · Giving · Business · Other

## Budget Frameworks Used

| Framework          | Split                                      |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| 50/30/20 Rule      | 50% needs / 30% wants / 20% savings & debt |
| Zero-Based         | Every dollar assigned a job                |
| Pay Yourself First | Save first, spend the rest                 |

## Key Insight Triggers

- Subscriptions charged 3+ times with no clear use → cancellation candidates
- Dining delivery > 25% of food budget → high-savings area
- Housing > 40% of income → cost burden flag
- No savings category visible → call out explicitly
